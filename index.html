About SCOUT

SCOUT (Smart Comparison & Ownership Understanding Tool) is independent. No manufacturer relationships, no dealer partnerships, no sponsored content — ever. Grades are based on IIHS safety data, NICB/HLDI theft statistics, RepairPal reliability scores, NHTSA recall data, owner forums, and real-world maintenance records. VERA (Vehicle Expert & Research Assistant) is the AI intelligence layer powered by Anthropic. Built by one person who got tired of biased car advice.
